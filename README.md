# Sales Performance Dashboard Summary

**Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales performance across three key areas: Sales Overview, Product & Customer Analysis, and Returns & Performance Analysis. The dashboard integrates multiple datasets (Sales, Products, Customers, Returns, and Targets) to deliver actionable business insights through KPIs, visualizations, and interactive filters.
 
**Key Business Insights
1. Revenue Concentration
A significant portion of total revenue is driven by the top 5 products – Camera, Laptop, Shoes, Table and T-shirt. This indicates that business performance is highly dependent on a few key contributors.
2. Preferred Channel
Sales are almost equally distributed between Retail and Online channels, indicating that customers actively engage with both purchasing modes. This suggests the importance of maintaining a strong presence and consistent experience across both channels to effectively meet customer preferences.
3. Sales vs Performance Gap
While overall sales show positive trends, high return rates and refund amounts reduce net revenue. This indicates that increased sales volume does not always translate into improved business performance.

**Risk Factors
1. High Return Rates in Specific Categories
Certain product categories demonstrate consistently high return rates, suggesting potential issues related to product quality, customer expectations, or delivery processes.
2. Dependency on Key Contributors
Revenue is heavily reliant on top-performing products, regions, and customers. This creates a business risk, as any decline in these areas could significantly impact overall revenue.
 

**Recommendations
1. Improve Product Quality and Return Management
Focus on identifying root causes of returns in high-return categories and implement corrective measures to reduce refund impact and enhance customer satisfaction.
2. Diversify Revenue Streams
Strengthen underperforming products and regions through targeted promotions and strategic initiatives to reduce dependency on top contributors.
3. Leverage High-Value Customer Segments
Develop targeted marketing campaigns, loyalty programs, and personalized offers to retain and expand high-value customer segments.
